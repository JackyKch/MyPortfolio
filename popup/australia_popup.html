Travel Plan

Back in July 2019, as part of my studies, I landed, for a semester, in Sydney, Australia. Here started one of the best experiences in my life. I got to explore Sydney and its surroundings but also Cairns, Brisbane, Byron Bay, Gold Coast and the Whitsundays and shot some pictures with my phone.
